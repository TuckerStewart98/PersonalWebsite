About me

  Tucker Stewart is an AI Researcher and Data Scientist. Currently, he is a PhD candidate in Computer Science and Systems with a focus in Data Science at the School of Engineering and Technology at the University of Washington Tacoma, under the supervision of Dr. Juhua Hu. Passionate about solving current challenges in AI and machine learning, he develops new methodologies that enhance the capabilities of AI systems. Research interests include time-series and sequential modeling, early rare event detection, and representation learning, with a focus on applications in healthcare.

  Tucker earned his Bachelor of Science in Computer and Systems with a minor in Mathematics, and Master of Science in Computer Science and Systems with a focus in Data Science, from the University of Washington Tacoma in June 2020 and March 2022, respectively.
